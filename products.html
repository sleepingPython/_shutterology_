Our products

Plantation shutters tailored to your windows, your style, and your budget.

Wood shutters

Premium hardwood shutters bring natural warmth and timeless elegance. Ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, and dining areas where beauty matters most.

Available in a range of painted and stained finishes. Custom louvers from 2½" to 4½".

Vinyl shutters

Durable, moisture-resistant vinyl is perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic spaces. Easy to clean and built to last.

Excellent value without compromising on look or fit. Multiple white and off-white tones available.

Special shapes

Arches, circles, triangles, and angled windows — we craft shutters for unusual openings that standard blinds can't handle.

Every specialty shape is templated on site for a seamless result.

Options & features

  • Traditional and hidden tilt rods
  • Full height, tier-on-tier, and café style
  • Bi-fold and bypass track systems
  • UV-resistant finishes
  • Child-safe designs
  • Professional measure & install included
