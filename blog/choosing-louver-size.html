Choosing the right louver size

Louver width affects how much light enters the room, how modern or traditional the shutters look, and how they scale with your window size.

2½" louvers

A classic choice that suits traditional and cottage-style homes. More louvers per panel create a finer, detailed appearance. Works well on smaller windows.

3½" louvers

The most popular size — a versatile balance of light, view, and contemporary style. Ideal for average-sized windows in most rooms.

4½" louvers

Larger louvers suit big windows and open-plan spaces. They feel modern and maximise the view when open, with fewer horizontal lines across the panel.

What we recommend

During your consultation we'll consider ceiling height, window dimensions, and your interior style. There's no single “correct” size — only what looks and performs best in your space.
