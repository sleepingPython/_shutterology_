How to care for plantation shutters

Plantation shutters are low maintenance by design. A few simple habits will keep them looking crisp for years.

Regular dusting

Use a soft microfibre cloth or duster on louvers and frames weekly. Tilt louvers closed first, dust one side, then the other.

Deeper cleaning

For fingerprints or kitchen grease, wipe with a slightly damp cloth and mild soap. Dry immediately. Avoid harsh chemicals, abrasive pads, and excess water on wood.

Vinyl shutters

Vinyl tolerates a bit more moisture — still avoid soaking hinges or mounting points. A gentle all-purpose cleaner is usually fine.

Check hinges annually

If a panel feels stiff, contact us for adjustment. Small tweaks prevent wear and keep panels aligned.
